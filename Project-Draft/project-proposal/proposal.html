Study Abroad Planner

Problem/Data Domain

The problem we aim to address is the lack of preparation many students face before studying abroad. For many, this is their first time leaving their home country, or traveling alone. Feeling supported during this transition is critical, yet the preparation stage is often overlooked. Planning is one of the best ways to help alleviate pre-departure anxiety. Currently, there is no centralized platform tailored specifically to help students organize and plan their study abroad experience. Packing lists and to-do items are often scattered across apps like Notes or Notion, while timelines are tracked separately on Google or Apple Calendar. Travel journaling is done inconsistently across physical notebooks or various digital platforms.

Despite the abundance of travel tools available, none are designed with the unique needs of study abroad students in mind—especially those navigating international travel for the first time. AroundMe is an app geared towards travellers who are unfamiliar with the location they're in, as study abroad students would initially be. It allows students to find banks, restaurants, and accommodation in a new city. It's a good way to support students once they're in their study abroad location, but is sorely lacking in ways to help them prepare beforehand. PackPoint is an app that's useful for making a packing list for short term trips, but isn't as suited for long study abroad trips. We plan to build a platform that allows students to feel confident and supported pre-departure by allowing them to interactively plan their trip details and document the experience.

App Description

This application is designed for underrepresented students interested in studying abroad. Users include first-generation college students, low-income students, students of color, and others who have historically faced barriers to international education. The app provides a streamlined way to explore programs, connect with peer mentors, and access scholarships and support resources.

Interactive Features

  • Timeline/Trip Planner (Big Feature): Users can create and manage their itinerary by adding events, this includes adding trips, tours, or even information related to restaurants or flights into the calendar. This way, users can keep track of all the plans and details of the trip.
  • Packing and Preparation To-Do Lists (Big Feature): Users will use this to make personalized packing and preparation checklists. They will be able to add items and check them off as completed or leave them unchecked for not completed. Users can customize it however they want tailored to their destination.
  • Travel Journal/Blog (Half Feature): This feature lets users document their travel experience by making journal entries, uploading pictures, and categorizing posts by date, location, or events. They can search or filter entries to revisit specific memories easily.

Information Viewed

Most of the information on the app will be inputed by users, as they would interact with the features and add events, dates, make changes to the checklist, and create blogs themselves.

Solving the Problem

This app acts as a centralized platform for study abroad students, integrating multiple features that will assist with study abroad planning. It simplifies logistical coordination, keeping events organized, and ultimately reducing the stress and enhancing students' overall study abroad experience. This app ensures they are well prepared, organized, and able to focus meaningfully on their international experiences.
